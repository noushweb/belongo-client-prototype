BELONGO — INTERACTIVE PROTOTYPE (Guest Experience)
===================================================

Thanks for taking a look! This is a clickable, high-fidelity design
prototype of the Belongo booking platform — the real screens, real
layout, real interactions, just not connected to a live backend yet.

HOW TO OPEN
-----------
1. Unzip this folder, keeping everything together.
2. Double-click  index.html  — it opens a simple start page.
3. Click "Enter the prototype" to begin, or jump to any screen from
   the list on the start page.

Tip: it looks best in Google Chrome, and you'll want an internet
connection (the photographs load from the web). On a phone it's
fully responsive — try it there too.

WHAT'S INSIDE
-------------
The full guest journey, end to end:

  Home  →  Search  →  Property  →  Checkout  →  Booking confirmed

Plus the account area and supporting pages:
  • Sign in / create account  • My bookings  • Booking detail
  • Cancel & refund (with a live refund timeline)
  • Account settings  • Payment methods  • Write a review
  • About  • Contact & support  • For hosts
  • Privacy, Terms, Payment & Refund policy
  • An Arabic / right-to-left demo screen

HOW TO EXPLORE
--------------
Most things are clickable — search, property cards, "Reserve",
"Pay", the menus. Follow the main journey first, then poke around.
A few secondary links are placeholders for now.

GOOD TO KNOW
------------
• These are design screens for review, not a transacting product —
  no real payments, accounts or data.
• A couple of host/admin screens are still in progress; the "For
  hosts" page is included so you can see the direction.

Questions or feedback? Just reply — happy to walk you through it.
