Payment & Refund Policy

Last updated 1 June 2026 · effective immediately

No hidden fees, no surprise conversions, no refund black holes. This is exactly what you pay, when, and what comes back if plans change.

✦ In plain English
  • ✓The total you see includes every fee and tax — nothing is added at the end.
  • ✓You’re charged in the currency shown; your bank’s rate (if any) is shown before you pay.
  • ✓Refunds follow a clear window and reach your card in about 3 days, tracked step-by-step.
  • ✓Reserve now, pay later: split into a deposit and a balance, with reminders — never a silent auto-cancel.

1 What you pay

The price on every search result, listing and checkout is the all-in total for your dates — already including the fees below. There is nothing extra at the end. The figure you confirm is the figure charged.

2 Every fee, explained

Here’s exactly what makes up a typical total — using a 3-night Dubai stay as an example. Each line is always itemised in your price breakdown:

LineWhat it isExample
AccommodationThe host’s nightly rate × nights (after any deal)AED 804
Cleaning feeOne-time, set by the hostAED 60
Service feeBelongo’s flat fee for running the platform & supportAED 48
Tourism dirham & VATGovernment charges, where applicableAED 40
TotalWhat you pay — nothing laterAED 952

3 When you’re charged

For a standard booking, the full total is taken when you confirm. Your price is locked for 10 minutes from the moment checkout begins, so demand pricing can’t change it while you enter details.

4 Reserve now, pay later

On eligible stays you can reserve with a part-payment: a deposit today secures the booking, and the balance is due on a stated date. We send two reminders (a week and two days before) and only then auto-charge your saved card — there is never a silent cancellation. You can pay the balance early anytime.

5 Refund windows

Each listing shows its policy at booking. The common structure:

Full refund
100%
Cancel within the free window (e.g. up to 48 h before check-in).
Partial
−1 night
After the free window, the first night is non-refundable; the rest is returned.
Non-refundable rate
Lower price
Some stays offer a cheaper, non-refundable rate — clearly labelled before you book.

Whatever applies, the exact amount and timeline are shown before you confirm a cancellation — see the refund tracker.

6 How refunds reach you

Refunds return to your original payment method. The flow: requested (instant) → approved (automatic, within 24 h) → sent to your bank → in your account. Most land within 3 days; some banks take up to 5. You can watch every step with dates, and if your bank is slow we’ll chase it with you.

7 Currency

You choose your display currency, and that is the currency we charge. For cards in another currency, your bank may apply its own conversion — we show an estimate of that on the checkout and summary, so it’s never a surprise on your statement. Local methods (mada, Apple Pay) avoid cross-border conversion entirely.

8 Failed payments

Cross-border declines are common and rarely about your balance. If a card is declined, your booking is held for 30 minutes while you try another method — a different card, Apple Pay, mada or Tabby — or reach a human who can hold it longer. You won’t lose the booking or the held price.
