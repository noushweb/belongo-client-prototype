1 Using Belongo

Belongo is a booking platform connecting guests with property hosts across the Gulf and beyond. You may use it if you’re 18 or older and can enter a binding contract. You’re responsible for keeping your account secure — turn on two-factor authentication for an extra layer.

You can browse and book as a guest without an account; creating one simply keeps your trips, payment methods and member rates in one place.

2 Bookings & the contract

When you confirm a booking, you enter a contract with the host for that stay; Belongo facilitates it, handles payment, and supports you throughout. Availability is shown in real time and re-checked at checkout — in the rare event a unit is taken between, we’ll find you an equivalent or refund in full.

Your confirmation email and booking reference are proof of the contract. If a payment page ever times out, your confirmation still arrives by email — you never lose a confirmed stay.

3 Prices & payment

Every price shown is the all-in total for your dates — accommodation, cleaning, service and taxes included — in your selected currency. The amount displayed is the amount charged. Once you begin checkout, we hold that price for 10 minutes so demand pricing can’t move it under you.

Full details of fees, part-payments, currency and refunds are in the Payment & Refund Policy, which forms part of these terms.

4 Changes & cancellations

Each listing states its cancellation window. Within the free window you receive a full refund; after it, the terms shown at booking apply. You can also modify your dates yourself, subject to availability and any price difference — shown before you confirm.

Refund amounts and timelines are always shown before you confirm a cancellation, and tracked step-by-step afterwards.

5 Guest conduct

Treat each property and host with respect: follow house rules, don’t exceed guest counts, no parties unless explicitly allowed, and leave the place as you found it. Hosts may decline or report bookings that breach these basics.

6 Reviews & content

Reviews must come from real, completed stays and reflect your honest experience. The same goes for “Nearby, by guests” contributions — photos and distances you add must be accurate, since other guests rely on them. We may remove content that’s fake, abusive or unlawful. You keep ownership of what you post but grant Belongo a licence to display it.

7 Liability

We work hard to keep listings accurate and stays trouble-free, but Belongo isn’t the property owner. To the extent the law allows, our liability is limited to the value of the affected booking. Nothing here limits rights you have under applicable consumer law.

8 Changes & governing law

We may update these terms; we’ll post the date above and, for material changes, notify you. These terms are governed by the laws of the United Arab Emirates, without affecting mandatory protections in your country of residence.
