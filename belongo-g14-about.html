Our story

Started by travellers who were tired of surprises

Belongo began with a familiar frustration: a price that grew at checkout, a “sea view” that wasn’t, and a refund that vanished into a support queue. Booking across the Gulf — Dubai one month, Amman the next — those small betrayals added up.

So we set three rules and built everything around them: show the total price always, keep every promise the listing makes, and put a real person one tap away when it matters. The technology — AI that reads reviews, dynamic pricing, a multi-country dashboard for hosts — all serves those three rules, never the other way around.

The map the maps forgot — filled in by travellers

The neighbourhood mosque, the late-night market, the quiet temple two streets over — the places that decide whether a stay works for you often aren’t on any map. So our guests put them there: real photos, real walking distances, confirmed by people who actually stayed.
